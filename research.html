Presentations

The IMA Lab held a webinar on the OSCI Toolkit via MCNPro in 2017. As part of the session, we presented a timeline of the toolkit within the context of the Getty's digital publishing initiative, discussed different approaches taken towards digital publishing by various institutions, and demoed core toolkit functionality.

http://mcn.edu/mcnpro-digital-publication-osci-toolkit/

Research

Selected papers on the OSCI Toolkit and the broader initiative. This is not meant to be a full bibliography, but rather, a cross-section that illustrates how different institutions approached the issue of scholarly digital publishing.
